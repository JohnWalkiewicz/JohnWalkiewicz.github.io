About the Project!

My 2017 Capstone Project for APP Academy consisted of several scenes, each representing a season of the year. I used shapes and animations in JavaScript. I enjoyed making a scene for each season by just using polygons. In addition, there were no boundaries on the project; I could make anything and everything. My favorite scene would have to be the winter scene since I believe that was the most creative and also took the most time to complete. The best part about the project was finally seeing the finished product. You can look at the project HERE!
