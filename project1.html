About the Project!

One APP Academy project that I believe I put the most effort into is my P5.js game "Legendary Battle Fields." This is a two player game in which each player starts with two random sets of cards from a standard deck. Player 1 clicks one of their cards and player 2 clicks one of their cards; whosever card is higher wins. The losing player's card is removed, and that player loses 5 points. I experimented with a JavaScript concept called P5.js in this project and although I had some difficulties in the beginning, I was able to get the basic version of the game working.
